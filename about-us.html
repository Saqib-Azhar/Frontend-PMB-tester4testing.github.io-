About us

Premier Solutions Inc. is a pioneer in the field of marketing products. We offer all kinds of custom printed items, from boxes to letterheads, from folders to stickers, from business cards to banners and absolutely anything you can think of that falls under the category of customized printing. Our aim is to cater to businesses of all sizes, from start-ups to multinationals, and help them market their products effectively. Everything is tailor-made and ordered to meet the individual demand of each customer; from the size and the style, to the printing and quantity we offer flexibility and high-quality coupled with competitive prices. In addition to custom printing we offer SEO and online marketing services as well. We help our clients promote their company and products in a more efficient way by conducting a full analysis of the current situation of the company and its online presence and coming up with strategies to overcome the problems which are preventing a company from growing in size. Basically we are a one-stop shop for all your marketing needs.

We at www.printmybox.com consider packaging to more than simply a means to encase products. We have taken it to a whole new level by introducing customized design. This enables our customers to develop their own designs and create tailored boxes that represent their brand as well as their products. Selecting your own size, design, finish etc gives the customers the power to choose each and every single aspect of the box’s design themselves.

Mr. Mian the CEO of Premier Solutions Inc. started the company some 15 years back with a vision of offering customized packaging to businesses. Since packaging is an integral part of marketing nowadays with companies constantly trying to push forward and gain customers’ attention. The focus initially was on start-up businesses which remains the core of our company to this day. It is easy for big companies to get customized boxes at low rates due to their bulk orders, but firms that are just starting up have very few options with fewer means of funds and lack of bulk discounts. Mr. Mian set up the company to specifically help out start-ups by offering a minimum order quantity of a mere 100 boxes. To this day the tradition of a helpful service and special discounts to start-ups is maintained.

At www.printmybox.com we completely stream line the process of placing a custom order making it hassle free. Our helpful customer representatives will guide you through the entire process from the initial phase of getting a custom quote to the final phase of getting a proof which leads to approval and payment for the order.

Steps Involved In Placing The Order

JUST THREE SIMPLE STEPS IN ORDER TO ACHIEVE THE PACKAGING OF YOUR DREAMS

Customer provides the specifications for the boxes they are looking for while we provide the price quotes accordingly.
Customer provides the artwork or design instructions and our team of designers create a digital proof to represent the final version of the box.
Once the digital proof is approved the customer pays as per the invoice and the production begins!
